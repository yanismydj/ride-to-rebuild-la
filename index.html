Supporting Our Community After the Devastating Fires

The January 2025 Palisades and Eaton fires, fueled by 100mph Santa Ana winds, have devastated our community. With over 37,000 acres burned, 11,000 structures destroyed, and 25 lives lost, more than 200,000 of our neighbors need our help to rebuild. Every mile you ride helps provide shelter and hope to those who lost everything in these unprecedented fires.

Note: To ensure transparency and security, we are not handling any donations directly. Please donate to Habitat for Humanity's Rebuild LA Fund here: Donate to Rebuild LA Fund

Event Details

  • Date: Saturday, February 1, 2025
  • Time: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Location: Westchester Parkway, Los Angeles
  • Distance: Do as many laps as you can! Each lap is about 4.5 miles (22 laps = 100 miles). Everyone welcome!
  • Ciele Hat: First 20 attendees will receive a free Ciele hat!
  • Support: SAG support area near the fire station with snacks, sports drink, Cadence, and Skratch
  • Mechanical Support: 2 Peaks Bikes from Westwood will provide a tent with bike stand for minor repairs
  • Special Support: 2 Peaks Bikes will provide gift cards for fire victims
  • Donation: $1 per lap completed - all proceeds go to wildfire victims

The Route - Laps of Westchester Parkway

Habitat for Humanity's Rebuild LA Fund

The Palisades and Eaton fires have devastated communities from Pacific Palisades to Pasadena, destroying over 11,000 homes and structures. All proceeds from this event will go directly to Habitat for Humanity's Rebuild LA Fund, helping to rebuild homes for fire victims across Los Angeles County. Together, we can help our neighbors rebuild their lives and make our community stronger than ever.
